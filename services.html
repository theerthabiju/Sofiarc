Our Services

Our sofiarc Specialties
Service

At Sofiarc, we provide compassionate, person-centred support services tailored to individual needs and goals. Our approach focuses on dignity, respect, and empowerment — ensuring every participant receives care that enhances daily living and overall wellbeing.

We understand that every individual’s journey is unique. Our services are flexible, responsive, and designed to adapt to changing needs. We work closely with participants, families, and support networks to deliver care that promotes confidence, independence, and meaningful participation in everyday life.

Personal Care Support


Respectful support with personal tasks such as hygiene, grooming, and daily routines, delivered with dignity and sensitivity.

Complex Care Support


Specialised, compassionate care for individuals with complex health or support needs, delivered safely, respectfully, and in line with professional care standards.

Assistance with Daily Tasks in Group / Shared Living


Support with everyday activities in shared living environments, promoting independence, cooperation, and a comfortable home atmosphere.

Community Nursing Care


Professional nursing support provided in the community to assist with medical needs while ensuring comfort, dignity, and wellbeing.

Community Participation Support


Encouraging active involvement in social, recreational, and community activities to build confidence, connection, and a sense of belonging.

Household Task Assistance


Help with household tasks to maintain a clean, safe, and organised living environment that supports daily comfort and wellbeing.

Travel & Transportation Support


Reliable transport assistance to help participants attend appointments, activities, and community engagements safely and on time.

At Sofiarc, we deliver trusted, personalised care with compassion and respect—supporting individuals and families to live meaningful, fulfilling lives.
